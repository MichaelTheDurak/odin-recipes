Apple Cider

Description

Homemade apple cider is tasty, reasonably healthy, and smells up the kitchen real nice. I like to make it during the holidays.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Allspice (whole)
  • Cloves

Steps

  1. Slice apples
  2. Add apple slices and all other ingredients to pot
  3. Fill pot with water
  4. Cover and boil for 30-60min
  5. Lower heat and simmer until remaining liquid barely covers apples
  6. Serve warm or remove from heat and chill
