Popcorn

Description

I pop my corn in an airpopper and season it heavy. This is how I season it, but the principle can be applied to any idividual taste.

Ingredients

  • Popcorn Kernels
  • Light olive oil
  • Old Bay
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Black Lime

Steps

  1. Pop corn in airpopper
  2. Drizzle olive oil and toss
  3. Add Old Bay (or other ingredient) and toss
  4. Rotate bowl 90 degrees
  5. Continue adding ingreients one-by-one, tossing and rotating the bowl inbetween each
